Temperature

Control the temperature of your house, set timetables, or disable temperature control completely.

Security

Enable and disable the alarms in your home, also control the locks on doors, you can set locks for each door.

Surveillance

View your home through cameras set up in the house. You can enable / disable any camera as well as look back on up to 3 days of footage.

Lighting

Control the lighting in your house, set timetables, or disable lighting control completely.

Profiles

Choose from a selection of default profiles or from ones that you create. These are based on timetables and settings that you choose.

Settings

View and change your accounts settings.
